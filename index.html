About Elmo

Elmo is a furry red monster from Sesame Street. He is the main character on Sesame Street and spends much of his time playing

Biography

Elmo has red fur and an orange nose.

Interests

Elmo loves to sing, dance, and play with his friends. He often says, "Elmo loves you!"

Friends

Elmo has a variety of friends, like Big Bird, Cookie Monster, and Oscar the Grouch.

Hobbies

Elmo enjoys playing with his pet goldfish, Dorothy. He also likes drawing and telling stories.

Fun Facts

Here are some fun facts about Elmo:

  • Elmo's favorite color is red.
  • He loves pizza.
  • Elmo has his own song called "Elmo's Song."
